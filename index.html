About Us



My name is Chaker. I am the founder of BackWatcher and a data analytics engineer in Paris.

As I spend so many hours on my PC I noticed that I have developed a hunchback posture. I didn't find a satisfying solution so I decided to use my useless webcam to remind me to stop slouching. Instead of using a small personal executable, I thought that many people could be in the same situation. So I decided to develop a distributable and secured app for everyone.

As using a webcam for an app is sensitive. I made the app works locally on your Mac. I also expect to make it certified to increase confidence in the code of the app I coded. Stay tuned for the next updates.
