How can I verify or control the network traffic?
You can fully control this with your own tools. For example, you can block or restrict BackWatcher in your firewall, only allowing it online when you activate your license. You can also use network monitors such as Little Snitch or Lulu on macOS, or GlassWire or Wireshark to see every outbound connection from the app and confirm that nothing else is sent.
